## What We Do

Precision CNC machining job shop serving the petrochemical, oil & gas, and industrial sectors along the Texas Gulf Coast. We make replacement parts, custom fittings, flanges, valve components, pump shafts, and small production runs.

Core capabilities:
- CNC turning up to 24" diameter, 80" length
- CNC milling, 3- and 4-axis
- Manual machining for one-off repair work
- Materials: carbon steel, stainless (304/316), Inconel, Monel, brass, bronze, some plastics
- In-house QC with CMM inspection, material certs available on request
