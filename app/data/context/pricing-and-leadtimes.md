# Pricing & Lead Times — Gulf Coast Machining Co.

*Internal reference for quoting. Updated April 2026.*

## Standard Lead Times

| Work type | Standard | During turnaround season |
|---|---|---|
| Stock fittings (catalog items) | 3–5 business days | 5–8 business days |
| Custom parts, simple (1–5 pcs) | 1–2 weeks | 2–3 weeks |
| Custom parts, complex (tight tolerance, exotic material) | 3–4 weeks | 4–6 weeks |
| Production runs (25+ pcs) | 4–6 weeks | quote individually |
| Emergency / plant-down | 24–72 hours when possible | 24–72 hours (we prioritize plant-down) |

## Rush Policy

- Emergency plant-down work jumps the queue. 35% expedite fee, waived for Sabine River Chemical per contract.
- Rush requests from non-plant-down customers: 25% expedite fee if we can fit it, honest "no" if we can't.
- Never promise a date the shop can't hit. Dale's rule: "A late part is worse than an honest no."

## Pricing Guidance

- Shop rate: $115/hr machining, $95/hr manual work
- Material at cost + 15%
- Minimum order: $250
- Quotes valid 30 days; exotic material quotes (Inconel, Monel) valid 10 days due to price volatility
- Repeat parts: we keep programs and setups on file, ~20% cheaper than first run

## Payment Terms

- Established accounts: NET 30
- New customers: 50% deposit on orders over $5,000; credit card or check on first order
- Past-due accounts: friendly reminder at 35 days, Dale calls personally at 45
